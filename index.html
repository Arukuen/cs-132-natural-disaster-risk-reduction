Hi. We are ELEMENTS.

And this is our project, Natural accidents make vacant pavements due to internal displacements, a study on how the natural disaster risk reduction policies of the Philippines have affected the country, as well as how effective this is in comparison to other countries. In this study, we aim to look at trends relating to forced internal displacement due to natural disasters of the 21st century using data from the Philippines, along with other South-east Asian countries.

Let's take a look at our data

Data

Source

The dataset was collected from the Global Internal Displacement Database from the Internal Displacement Monitoring Centre.

Data Collection Process

The data was obtained from querying the entire database relating to internal displacement from disasters, from the countries listed, and from the range of 2008-2022.

Data set Size

This resulted in an Excel file with 2567 events of internal displacement.

Data Preprocessing

We remove columns such as Event name, Date of Event, Disaster Internal Displacements, Hazard Category, Hazard Sub Type, and Event Codes (Code:Type).

Present Columns

This leads to the dataset only having the columns ISO3, Country / Territory, Year, Disaster Internal Displacements (Raw), and Hazard Type.
